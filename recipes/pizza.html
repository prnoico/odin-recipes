Pizza

Description:

The Greatest pizza you have ever seen!

Ingredients:

  • water
  • flour
  • salt
  • yeast
  • tomatoes
  • mozzarella
  • basil
  • oregano
  • tomatoes
  • your favourite addings

Steps:

  1. Add yeast to a warm water.
  2. Combine watered yeast with flour and salt.
  3. Make a dough.
  4. Let it rest for few hours.
  5. Now you can make sauce by boiling tomatoes with basil and oregano.
  6. When dough is ready form pizza shape dough and add ingredients.
  7. Cook it in the oven for 15 min at 300 C
