Tomato Spaghetti

Description:

The Greatest spaghetti you have ever seen!

Ingredients:

  • spaghetti
  • fresh tomatoes
  • chilli
  • basil
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Cut tomatoes.
  2. Add spaghetti to the boiling water.
  3. Pour olive oil on the pan.
  4. Add garlic and chilli to the hot oil.
  5. After 1 minute add tomatoes and fresh basil.
  6. Boil it for 25 minutes.
