Sandwitch

Description:

The Greatest sandwitch you have ever seen!

Ingredients:

  • bread
  • butter
  • cheese
  • ham
  • mayo
  • cucumber

Steps:

  1. Smear slice of bread with butter.
  2. Add slice of cheese and ham.
  3. Add mayo.
  4. Add two slices of cucumber.
  5. Close the sandwitch with another slice of bread.
